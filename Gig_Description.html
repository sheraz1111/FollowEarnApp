⚙️ How the System Works

  • ✅

    Create Campaign

    A user wants followers on YouTube/TikTok. They pay you to buy coins and post their demand (campaign).

  • ✅

    Complete Tasks

    Another user clicks on the campaign to earn free coins, subscribes to the channel, and submits a Screenshot as proof.

  • ✅

    Approval System

    The user who created the campaign views the screenshot in their dashboard and approves it. The task-doer instantly receives their coins!

  • ✅

    Platform Profit

    Throughout this entire transaction loop, the app owner (YOU) continues to receive a passive profit margin!
